Local teen found dead after overdose

July 10th, 2023, 4:33 PM
Alex Dube,

NARRAGANSETT, R.I. (WLNE) — Early Sunday morning, a teenage girl died late on Saturday night from a fatal overdose, according to the Narragansett Police Department.

In an official statement department officials verified that the 17-year-old had passed away at a residence on Old Pine Road in the early hours of the morning.

The Narragansett Police Department advised parents on Sunday to advise young teenagers about the affects of the abuse of over the counter medications. However at this time it has been unconfirmed that this death is directly related.

Over-the-counter medicine abuse has risen among adolescents in recent years, and can result in serious heart problems, seizures, coma, or even death, the US Food and Drug Administration said in a 2020 warning to the public.

Within their official statement, the Narragansett Police Department verified that the death had been ruled as a suicide and would be investigated as such.
